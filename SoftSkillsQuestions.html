VOD - TV Application developer

  • Situation: I worked as a C++ software engineer for a telecommunications company (mirada)in the company's product called Inspire. There were two components: a middleware and a GUI written using c++, qt and qml. The Middleware was used decouple our implementations from different STB providers and to implement new functionality that was not provided by other third parties such directly by us such as VOD support multimedia, PVR... The GUI was the product built on the top of the middleware using MVC.
  • Task: Implement VOD support in the company's middleware. At first RTSP. Later HLS was also required for Fox and HBO VOD catalog. So far implementation just supported traditional cable TV but no VOD protocols over the network. Nobody had experience with this technologies within the company.
  • Action: I was responsible of designing, developing and even integrating on customer site of these VOD features. Our video player in the middleware had to be decouple from the low level details of every protocol specific implementation since it could play vod, tv, multimedia video/audio. Within VOD you had to support both protocols. To solve this we used strategy and template method patterns to have an abstraction that allow us to have shared logic between all the different playback mecahism that the lower layers had to deal with. Also state pattern was used to implement the RTSP and HLS protocols. Also the GUI was extended to support the specific requirements of the VOD player.
  • Result: We manage to deploy to production our RTSP solution for Cablevision in Monterrey and Televisa in Mexico City. Some of the largest tv networks in Mexico under the name of Izzi. HLS solution was deployed as a pilot a small scale with Fox and HBO integration, again,for Cablecom and Televisa.
